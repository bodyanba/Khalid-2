About US
Hello Future...

Under the blessings of His Majesty Sultan Qaboos bin Said, the Diwan of Royal Court is delivering the National Youth Program for Skills Development reflecting His Majesty’s interest in and the national priority he places upon the Youth of Oman.

In response to a world where technology is offering unparalleled opportunities to societies and communities, Oman is well positioned to take full advantage to ride these waves of evolution and create its own waves of innovation. Of course, this needs the right skills to grasp the opportunities - which is where the National Youth Program for Skills Development is set to make a significant difference with its vision to create a community of young Omanis ready to embrace and exploit the opportunities of the 4th Industrial Revolution for the benefit of Omani society - and for future economic prosperity.

Welcome Message

His Excellency Dr Ali Qassim Jawad Al-Lawati Advisor for Studies and Research to the Diwan of Royal Court Chairman, The National Youth Program for Skills Development

A very warm welcome to the National Youth Program for Skills Development. This program has been designed and developed with the direct blessing of His Majesty the Sultan who has always given the highest priority to the youth of Oman, right from the country’s renaissance to today’s knowledge-based economy.

As we are now experiencing the challenges and opportunities presented by the 4th Industrial Revolution (4IR), His Excellency the Minister of the Diwan has instructed we implement His Majesty’s vision and so to equip the youth of Oman with the latest relevant attitudes, skills and knowledge through the National Youth Program for Skills Development. In so doing we will build a generation of Omanis equipped with the capabilities to exploit the opportunities of the 4IR and so deliver a 4IR knowledge-based society.

Living in a technology-dominated world is now our current reality. So join us on the amazing learning journey that the National Youth Program for Skills Development offers – a journey where we will immerse you in the latest 4IR trends, technologies and applications – and so prepare you to be part of a future where Oman continues to grow and prosper.

Please review the National Youth Program for Skills Development website, read the application criteria carefully and follow the simple steps to apply. I wish you all the best and believe that you will benefit enormously from this experience.

Our Vision is to create a community of young Omanis ready to embrace and exploit the opportunities of the 4th Industrial Revolution

Let’s

Equip young Omanis with the skills, attitudes and knowledge of 4IR

Build a community of young Omanis working together to become 4IR ambassadors for the Sultanate - for our Oman

Harness knowledge and enthusiasm to develop 4IR capability and expertise for the wellbeing of Oman - and its future economic prosperity

Advisory Cmmittee

The program is designed specifically for the youth; and so the National Program for Skills Development formed an advisory committee consisting of a group of youth aged between 15 and 29 years old. Their role is to provide suggestions and innovative ideas, along with contributing in selecting the Program’s partners. The Committee is made up of youth from various walks of life in the Sultanate; as they are either studying at a school or university, or working in their own businesses or in different institutions, whether from the government or private sector.

Partners

The National Youth Program for Skills Development is keen on identifying the highest standards in selecting the Program’s partners; in order to provide the best experience that lives up to the aspirations of the Omani youth based on the 4th Industrial Revolution and its skills.

Therefore, we partnered with the educational and developmental institutions topping the international specialized classifications; in order to provide the latest scientific and technical approaches and practices in the world. Furthermore, engagements have been made with a number of leading Omani institutions that have enriched the Program with local experiences and achieved balance for the participants between the international and national experiences.
