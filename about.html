Welcome Message

His Excellency Dr Ali Qassim Jawad Al-Lawati Advisor for Studies and Research to the Diwan of Royal Court Chairman, The National Youth Program for Skills Development

A very warm welcome to the National Youth Program for Skills Development. This program has been designed and developed with the direct blessing of His Majesty the Sultan who has always given the highest priority to the youth of Oman, right from the country’s renaissance to today’s knowledge-based economy.

As we are now experiencing the challenges and opportunities presented by the 4th Industrial Revolution (4IR), His Excellency the Minister of the Diwan has instructed we implement His Majesty’s vision and so to equip

Our Vision is to create a community of young Omanis ready to embrace and exploit the opportunities of the 4th Industrial Revolution

Let’s

Equip young Omanis with the skills, attitudes and knowledge of 4IR

Build a community of young Omanis working together to become 4IR ambassadors for the Sultanate

Harness knowledge and enthusiasm to develop 4IR capability and expertise for the wellbeing of Oman

So the National Youth Program for Skills Development Advisory Committee has assembled a board aged between 15 and 29 years and drawn from all walks of life. Their role on the Advisory Committee is to provide advice and counsel to the National Youth Program for Skills Development Team.
