National Youth Program for Skills Development
In interpretation of the high keenness of His Majesty the Sultan Qaboos bin Said (may God protect him) on the youth, the Diwan of Royal Court launched a national initiative to strengthen the future skills of the youth; in order to keep up with the Sultanate's trends towards the 4th Industrial Revolution (4IR).

We will continue – with God’s willing - our full sponsorship of the Omani Youth, believing in the youth’s role in creating the present and future

From the speech of His Majesty Sultan Qaboos bin Said on the 15th National Day Anniversary.

Hello Future...

The Diwan of Royal Court welcomes you in the National Youth Program for Skills Development.

Welcome to the Initiative designed specifically for you, the emerging Omani youth and its promising future; in order to provide you with the skills, knowledge and values ​​of the 4th Industrial Revolution (4IR) to contribute in creating the present and the future ...

Al Nashia
Scheme

This Scheme targets the age group between 15-17 years old and is designed to give participants an impressive educational experience full of challenges and opportunities to provide them with skills of the future; such as programming, robotics, computational thinking, digital media, digital citizenship and other 21st century skills; thus contributing to enhancing their creativity and self-confidence to go through the 4th Industrial Revolution and its applications at global levels.

15-17 years old

21st century skills

150 participants

Find More
Al Shabab
Scheme

This Scheme targets the age group between 18-29 years old and is designed to give participants a unique educational experience to equip them with the (4IR) skills, values ​​and knowledge. It starts with a 3-month E-Learning Stage, where those who successfully complete the requirements will be qualified for the Omani Youth Tech Ideathon to develop ideas for the startup technology projects; followed by the Applied Learning Stage within and outside the Sultanate to benefit from the successful international experience before they work on the establishment of their startup technology projects on the ground and presenting them to the investors.

18-29 years old

tech-startup projects

1000 participants
