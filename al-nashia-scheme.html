Al Nashia Scheme
This is a 2 week intense residential program delivered in Muscat to 150 participants aged between 15-17 years old.
Rigorous and Impartial Assessment Process
Ministry of Education will identify 1000 potential applicants from the top students in Math and English, in Public and Private Schools all around Oman. After online and physical assessment process, a 150 successful applicants will be chosen for the 1st cohort of Al Nashia Scheme.
Rigorous and Impartial Selection Process
A 14 day intensive and challenging residential program taking place in Muscat from 4th to 17th August 2018
